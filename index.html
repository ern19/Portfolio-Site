My first independent project as a developer was a Hangman game. Playing Hangman on a sheet of paper is obviously a simple undertaking; programming one to work in a browser window or on a cell phone was a whole other story. I tried to maintain a simple, clean design, intuitive controls, as well as the ability to play it on mobile. In upcoming versions, I hope to implement more catergories for gameplay, and hopefully a fully deployed standalone app for IOS and Android.
